Adult & Elderly (20+ years) Vaccination Schedule

Vaccine When to Give Dosage Route Site
Influenza Vaccine Annually (Starting from 20 years) 0.5 ml Intra-muscular Upper arm
Hepatitis B Vaccine 3 doses (0, 1, and 6 months) 1 ml Intra-muscular Deltoid muscle
Pneumococcal Vaccine Once, or every 5 years for those at high risk (65+ years) 0.5 ml Intra-muscular Upper arm
Shingles Vaccine Once, starting at age 50 0.5 ml Intra-muscular Upper arm
Tetanus, Diphtheria & Pertussis (Tdap) Once, followed by Td every 10 years 0.5 ml Intra-muscular Upper arm
COVID-19 Vaccine 2 doses followed by boosters (depending on the vaccine) 0.5 ml Intra-muscular Upper arm

Why Vaccines Matter ?

  • Vaccines help to prevent the spread of dangerous diseases and maintain public health.
  • Immunization schedules are designed to ensure that individuals are protected at the right age.
  • Some vaccines require boosters to maintain immunity throughout life.
  • Vaccines are administered at different ages for maximum protection, from childhood through adulthood.
